First Time Reveal: Kylie Jenner Unveils her Sons Face and Name

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, August 15, 2024


For the first time, Kylie Jenner has shared her son’s face with the world and announced that his name is Aire.

The renowned makeup mogul welcomed her second child, a baby boy, with her boyfriend Travis Scott in February 2022. Shortly after the birth, they revealed his name as Wolf Webster.

However, just a few weeks later, Kylie made a surprising revelation that she had opted to change her son’s name, expressing that “it didn’t feel like it was him.”

Now, for the first time, she has publicly shared that his name is Aire.

Furthermore, this marks the debut of her son’s face to the public.
