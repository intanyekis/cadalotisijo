Kim Kardashian Flashes Breasts in Sheer Mesh Top in NYC: Picture

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, September 3, 2024

Hot mesh! Kim Kardashian attended legendary fashion and celebrity photographer Mario Testino's Alta Moda event on Monday, Nov. 18 in NYC wearing a netted, see-through, turtleneck top that left very little to the imagination.

Kanye West's fiancee, who has increasingly expressed interest in high fashion and attended couture shows in Paris with her beau back in October, had a more artistic interpretation of how winter white should be done at Monday's exhibit. The reality star, 33, strutted confidently in the sheer top with a strategically-placed, nude demi-cup bra beneath her mesh shirt, which still allowed her to flaunt ample cleavage.

The mom to 5-month-old North West neatly tucked her skin-baring number in to a body (and booty!)-hugging, cream-colored maxi skirt. She accessorized her outfit with Tom Ford spring 2012 chain-embellished sandals, and a satin frame clutch from Bottega Veneta for a Grecian-inspired touch. As for beauty, Kardashian swept her blonde locks back into a sleek ponytail, and added the finishing touch to her creative look with a rich, plum red lip color.

At the same event, in stark contrast to Kardashian was younger half-sister Kendall Jenner, 18, who also went white for the exhibit. The up and coming model opted for a fuzzy angora sweater with a V-cut back and black-and-white patterned pants.

Mario Testino is a world-renowned photographer who remains a Vogue staple; He has done portrait sessions for the Royal family, including Prince William and Kate Middleton's engagement shoot. His exhibit Alta Moda, which means "high fashion" in Spanish, captured Peruvians in the Cusco region of the country in their colorful, traditional garb. It opens at the Queen Sofia Spanish Institute on Wednesday, Nov. 20.

This is not Kardashian's first time flashing her top half in recent days. As first revealed by Us Weekly, Kardashian appears topless atop a motorcycle in West's "Bound 2" music video.
