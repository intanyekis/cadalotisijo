Big Brother Naija 2021 Final Week Voting Poll Results

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, July 6, 2024

It’s the final week of the 2021 Big Brother Naija Season 6. Conversations are heating up as to which housemate would be the BBNaija Shine Ya Eye winner. Even the Top 6 finalists are itching to know the odds beforehand.

The viewers are not left out in this curiosity tussle to know the chances that their favourite housemate emerges the winner. More than ever, your vote has never mattered more.

As the 72-day show comes to an end, questions have been building. This article would also try to address as many as possible. In case you have any, kindly comment your questions in the[wpdiscuz-feedback id=”67pfbw08qc” question=”Have Any Questions? Let us know” opened=”0″] comment section below[/wpdiscuz-feedback].

Who are the Remaining Housemates in BBNaija?

Of the 26 housemates that participated in the reality show, only 6 Housemates consisting of 2 females and 4 males made it to the final week of the 2021 Big Brother Naija show. The Top 6 Finalists remaining in the BBNaija House are:

  • Angel
  • Cross
  • Emmanuel
  • Liquorose
  • Pere
  • Whitemoney

    • Who has the Highest Votes In BBNaija 2021 Poll Today?

    RNN sent out a Poll on Monday to capture the voting decision of BBNaija viewers and measure the support each housemate would be getting. As of the time of filing this report, we have recorded a total of 331,900 votes from 322,048 Viewers of the 2021 BBNaija reality show.

    So far, Whitemoney has the highest votes in the 2021 BBNaija Final Week Voting Poll. But, that didn’t come off as a surprise. He has been the most talked-about housemate and the most voted each time he is up for eviction. Many viewers believed Whitemoney is the winner of BBN this season right from his first week in the house. Even his fellow housemates confessed to this. It seems they just might be right.

    Following him is Pere, Liquorose and Cross, while Emmanuel and Angel have the least number of votes.

    BBNaija 2021 Voting Results

    The table below illustrates how viewers voted in the Big Brother Naija Final Week Voting Poll.

    Well, we can’t pronounce the 2021 BBNaija Shine Ya Eye Winner, but Whitemoney does have the highest chance of emerging as the winner. But what can I say, the tide of things can still change in the limited time we have left to vote.

    The final week voting is open from 7 PM (GMT) on Monday, September 27, 2021. The 2021 BBNaija final voting ends on Friday, October 1, 2021, by 8 PM (GMT). To cast your vote, simply:

  • Visit the official BBNaija Website on Africa Magic https://africamagic.dstv.com/show/big-brother-naija/season/6/vote
  • Create An Account or Login to your existing account
  • Select your favourite housemate(s) and Vote

    • How to Predict the Winner of BBNaija 2021? – You predicted the winner of this year’s season by participating in the free Online BBN Poll.

    What’s the Grand Prize for 2021 BBNaija S6 Winner?

    This season’s BBNaija Shine Ya Eye winner will walk away with a whopping 90,000,000 Naira grand prize which includes a 30,000,000 Naira cash prize, cash in Abeg digital wallet, bitcoins courtesy Patricia, a two-bedroom apartment courtesy RevolutionPlus Property, a top of the range SUV from Nigerian automaker, Innoson Motors and a trip for two packaged by Travelbeta.

    Who is Big Brother (Biggie)?

    The identity of Big Brother is never revealed. As in all Big Brother shows around the world, the identity of Big Brother is a mystery and will remain so before, during and after the show.
