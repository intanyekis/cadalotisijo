    Falsesymmetry Is An English YouTuber

    Falsesymmetry, real name, Katy is an English Youtuber and a streamer. She is best known for her participation in the Minecraft server "Hermitcraft."

    Prior to her streaming career, she was a graphic designer. She is commonly referred to as False. 

    False joined HermitCraft in Season 2, and her first episode was uploaded on June 21, 2014.  She has participated in every HermitCraft season since then.

    Furthermore, she is also a part of Empires SMP which is a modified Minecraft server in which players create their own empires in order to trade goods and resources with other empires and wage wars.

    She debuted in Season 2 and established her steampunk-themed Empire on the side of a Mountain Biome. She portrays an amnesic tinker from another world who has mysteriously awoken in this one.

    In addition to streaming, she has competed in numerous MC Championship tournaments.

    The Noxcrew hosts a Minecraft Tournament for YouTubers and streamers in which 10 teams of four compete in a series of eight minigames chosen by the participants.

    Since her debut, she has taken part in every single event (except MCC16). She is currently the only female competitor with three victories.

    FalseSymmetry is well-known for being the event's first back-to-back winner.
