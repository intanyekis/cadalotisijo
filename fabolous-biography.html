Fabolous Bio (Wiki)

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, July 16, 2024

Who is Fabolous?

Fabolous is an American Rapper, actor, and Songwriter. He is famous for the soundtrack of  Blade II(2002), Coach Carter (2005), and The King of Staten Island(2020).

He made his self appearance in DJ Cassidy’s Pass the Mic: BET Awards Edition (TV Special) in 2021.

What is the birth name of Fabolous?

Fabolous was born on 18th November 1977 in Brooklyn, New York. His birth name was John David Jackson. There isn’t any information about his parent’s and siblings’ identities. David holds American Nationality with African- American ethnicity.

Moving towards his educational background, at the High school of Arts and Design. Besides that, he graduated from City-As-School High school.

Fabolous: Career

Fabolous released his first studio album, Ghetto Fabolous in 2001. In 2003, he released his second studio album, Street Dreams. Besides that, in the following year, he released his third studio album Real Talk.

Moreover, Fabolous launched his fourth studio album, From Nothin’ to Somethin’ in 2007. He released his fifth studio album, Loso’s Way in 2009.

Furthermore, in 2014 he break out his sixth studio album, The Young OG Project. He has a collaboration album with Jadakiss named Friday on Elm Street in 2017.

In addition, he released his seventh studio album, Summertime shootout 3: Coldest Summer Ever
in 2019.

Fabolous: Awards and Nominations

  • Fabolous won American Music Awards for Make Me Better (Rap Song) in 2008.
  • He is the nominee for two times Grammy Awards for Money Goes, Honey Stays (2010), and Dips It Low (2005).
