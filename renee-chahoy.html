Erkia Jayne's Mother Renee Chahoy Married Life. Know About Her Husband, Married Life, Age, Instagram

Renee Chahoy is a Hollywood actress, model, and classically trained pianist. Besides her profession, she is also known for being the mother of a famous American singer, television personality, and actress, Erika Nay Chahoy, aka Erika Jaynee.

The female artist was born on August 24, 1952. She favors keeping her personal life away from the public eye; thus, she has not disclosed anything about her family members except her mother. Her mother died of Alzheimer's in November 2014 at the age of 86. Concerning her education, she graduated in Music from Georgia State University.

Who's Actress Renee Chahoy Husband?

As of now, a classically trained pianist, Renee Chahoy, is enjoying her single life. Previously, she tied the knot with her husband, whose identity is still a mystery. To this day, she has not revealed anything related to her past marriage.

The former model separated from her husband when her daughter, Erika Jaynee, was just nine months old. Since then, she raised her daughter as a single mother, and currently, she is enjoying a proud motherhood life with her family. 

Being a celebrity and moreover a mom of a renowned celebrity, Chahoy still prefers to keep a low-key profile about her personal life. Therefore, the Georgia State University graduate is not available on social platforms like Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter.

Renee Chahoy's Daughter Erika Jaynee's Relationships

Renee Chahoy is not the only one to experience heartbreak in her relationship. Her child Erika Jaynee has undergone a similar situation in her life. 

Speaking about Jaynee's life, she was previously married to Thomas Zizzo. The duo met in New York City for the first time. After their first encounter, they began to share a romantic connection.

Later, in December 1991, the couple exchanged the marriage vows at St. Patrick's Cathedral. The duo lived a healthy and happy married life and shared a son, Thomas Zizzo Jr (Tommy Zizzo), born in 1994 from their relationship of husband and wife.

However, after the birth of Tommy, their relationship did not work well, and the husband-wife duo decided to split from each other. In 1996, they separated legally after five years of marriage.

50 aged Erika and 83 aged Thomas meet for the first time at a restaurant. After the first meeting, they just shared a friendship bond, and later they became lovers.
