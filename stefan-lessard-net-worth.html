Born on June 4, 1974, in Anaheim, California, Lessard was exposed to the world of music from an early age.

Personal Life

Stefan Lessard’s personal life has had its ups and downs, with significant milestones and unfortunate tragedies shaping his journey.

Tragically, in 1996, the couple experienced the devastating loss of their daughter Aslan to Sudden Infant Death Syndrome (SIDS). This unimaginable tragedy deeply impacted their lives.

After years of navigating the emotional challenges, Lessard and Baucom decided to part ways and divorced in 2004. However, life had more in store for Lessard.

In 2005, Lessard found love again and married Jaclyn Burton. Together, they built a beautiful family and have three daughters – Hazel, Flora, and India. The joy and love they share have brought new chapters of happiness to Lessard’s personal life.
