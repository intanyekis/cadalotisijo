Fans Are Unsure If Taylor Swift Lied To Joe Alwyn About Her Matt Healy Relationship

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, August 10, 2024

Highlights

  • Swift's fast transition from Alwyn to Healy sparked rumors of drama, leading fans to question their dynamic.
  • Alwyn was reportedly upset at how quickly Swift moved on, despite trusting her during her collaboration with Healy.
  • The trio's intertwined fate began when Swift met Healy in 2014, leading to speculations about her previous relationships and engagements.

The transition from Taylor Swift's relationship with Joe Alwyn to her short-lived romance with Matt Healy was almost scandalous due to how she quickly moved on from the former. It became more sensational when Alwyn reacted to the news of her relationship with Healy, making many wonder if there was some sort of dynamic among the trio.

Towards the final months of Taylor Swift's relationship with Joe Alywn, she and Matt Healy worked together on her 2022 album, "Midnights." Naturally, the actor was aware of the pair's collaboration and was reportedly fine with it. However, Alwyn appeared to retrace his steps after Swift and Healy's dating news, causing the public to wonder if the music star lied to him about the nature of her friendship with Healy. This article will examine the relationship dynamics that Taylor Swift had with Alwyn and Healy, as well as a look back at when she met the pair.

Did Taylor Swift's Relationship With Matt Healy Cause Tension With Joe Alwyn?

The most profound confirmation of Taylor Swift and Joe Alwyn's breakup was his absence when Swift kicked off her iconic Eras tour in March 2023. Despite writing over ten songs about the actor, he was nowhere to be found during her most anticipated tour. The separation was as quiet as the ultra-private six-year run of their relationship. Despite the undramatic manner in which Swift and Alwyn broke up, the final moments of their relationship ended up being sensational as fans wondered what went wrong. This was because Swift grew close to her former flame, Matt Healy, around that time. It is noteworthy that Swift started dating Healy a few weeks after breaking up with Alwyn.

The dynamic among the trio further piqued fans' curiosity due to Joe Alwyn's reaction to Taylor Swift and Matt Healy's dating news. According to the DailyMail, the "Stars at Noon" actor was quite upset that his ex-girlfriend moved on quickly. He was reportedly also unhappy because he put his trust in Swift when she collaborated with Healy. The insider revealed:

"Joe feels slighted and is distraught after seeing her budding relationship with Matt, but is doing his best to keep busy and focus on himself."

Taylor Swift appeared to have spent more time with Matt Healy as her relationship with Joe Alwyn drew to its end. Back in November 2022, the 1975 lead vocalist revealed that he and Swift worked together on her album, and he described her as "amazing." Two months later, in January 2023, the "Willow" crooner gave an impromptu performance at The 1975's concert at the O2 Arena in London. The insider who spoke to DailyMail stated that Alwyn was aware of the pair's collaboration. They noted:

"Joe was aware that Taylor and Matt were making music together and collaborating. She told him that they had become friends, and he trusted her."

The source further stated that there was no "overlap" between Taylor Swift's relationship with both men, but Joe Alwyn's reaction to how she quickly moved on showed something was amiss. The former couple broke up in April 2023, but leading up to that, Matt Healy seemed to be more present in her life. He promoted the Eras tour during the final leg of The 1975's tour. By May 2023, Swift was photographed kissing Healy during a dinner date in Manhattan.

When Did Taylor Swift Meet Joe Alywn And Matt Healy?

It appears the trio's fate was tied together in that the timeline of the first time they met is partly connected. Taylor Swift first met Matt Healy in 2014 during his concert. The pair exchanged numbers at the time, and Healy would later gush about meeting her in an interview. Insiders revealed to RadarOnline about the pair:

"It's very new, and they're having fun. They're seeing where things go. Taylor is absolutely gaga about Matt."

Despite the speculations of a brewing romance, Taylor Swift and Matt Healy moved on from each other. Two years later, in 2016, she became romantically linked with Joe Alwyn. This happened a few months after her breakup with Tom Hiddleston. However, according to Elle Magazine, it was not until 2017 before an insider confirmed that Swift and Alwyn were indeed an item. Before their breakup, many were convinced Swift and Alwyn were engaged.
