Did Justin Bieber Write Any Songs About Women Other Than Selena Gomez And Hailey Bieber?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, August 14, 2024

From "What Do You Mean" to "Peaches," Justin Bieber's songs are always popular. And most of his music is about love, heartbreak, and confusing emotions. Since Bieber dated Selena Gomez for years and is now married to Hailey Bieber, it's easy to guess that most of his songs are about these two relationships. However, the singer has dated a few other people. And given his huge music catalog, it seems likely that he penned at least a few tunes about some other women.

Who else has Justin Bieber written songs about? And what should fans know about these other relationships? While he has talked the most about Selena Gomez and Hailey Bieber, Justin Bieber's other romances are just as juicy. And they might have influenced some of Bieber's most beloved songs.

Did Justin Bieber Write A Song About Caitlin Beadles?

Usher and Justin Bieber kept in touch after Usher helped the singer get his start in the music business. And while he was being mentored by Usher in Atlanta, Bieber began dating Caitlin Beadles. Considering the massive success that Bieber has enjoyed for several decades now, it's easy to forget that he was once a young star just starting out.

E! News reported that Justin Bieber might have written his song "Never Let You Go" about Beadles.

The song didn't hit number 1 on the Billboard Hot 100 and was number 21, but that's still pretty good considering how many pop songs are released all the time.

According to E! News, Bieber and Beadles were in a relationship from 2008 to 2019. The publication reported that they split up as Bieber was becoming more well-known and that took its toll. Of course, they were both young at the time. It would have been tough to keep dating even if Bieber wasn't becoming a huge pop star.

Did Justin Bieber Write A Song About Sofia Richie?

Justin Bieber and Sofia Richie dated for a period of six weeks, according to People. A source explained to E! News, "They were never officially together, but more casual hanging out and having fun." The source continued, "Justin doesn't want a relationship right now, he wanted to cool things down."

While Justin Bieber and Sofia Richie's time together was highly publicized, it doesn't appear that he wrote a song about her.

Since they were only a couple for a brief period of time, Bieber might not have wanted to write about Richie. It seems that he has been the most influenced by his long-term relationships. And since he definitely seems like a romantic, that seems logical.
