General Hospital Spoilers April 22, 2024 Through April 26, 2024

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, August 7, 2024

Soaps.com has the latest, new General Hospital spoilers from Monday, April 22, to Friday, April 26. Dex finds himself facing doubts, while Josslyn winds up in yet another war of words with Kristina. Elsewhere, Sam takes a stand (only to later wonder if she’s doing more harm than good), and the pre-wedding festivities get under way. Plus, what on this planet could bring Carly and Nina together? All that and more in this week’s spoilers!

General Hospital spoilers week of April 22:

Weekly preview: Complications arise during Chase and Brook Lynn’s pre-wedding celebrations — and a cry for help sends [Spoiler] out the door!

General Hospital spoilers for Monday, April 22:’

In today’s, General Hospital recap, Nina accuses Ava of wanting Sonny to herself, Sam and Jason disagree about Danny, and Carly and Sonny argue over Jason.

Sonny and Carly fans may want to look away, because the couple — whom many hope to see reunite — are about to go at it. And not in the sexy way.

Nina makes a discovery, but will it be something she can use to her advantage?

Sam makes it clear to Jason that he is to stay far, far away from their son. (Wonder how Danny will feel about that?)

They haven’t spent much time together of late, but Drew is about to reconnect with Willow.

Lois proves she’s not the only member of her family who knows a thing or two about Deception. Maxie, get ready to be impressed!

As the soap leads up to the big Cody/Mac reveal, Josh Kelly books a new job — and his background before he became a daytime star makes him the perfect person for the role!

General Hospital spoilers for Tuesday April 23:

In today’s, General Hospital recap, Brook Lynn an Chase’s parties bring drama for Kristina, Joss and Dex.

As the countdown to Brook Lynn’s wedding begins, today her bridal shower kicks into high gear. Meanwhile, Chase’s bachelor party is also underway… and it involves the throwing of dangerous weapons as well as a most unexpected guest!

Tracy might be one of the most guarded people to ever reside in Port Charles. So who manages to bring her walls down a bit today?

Remember a few weeks back when Kristina and Josslyn got into a verbal altercation? Well, get ready, because here comes round two!

Fan favorite Sean Blakemore is set to take aim once again — and you won’t want to miss the details surrounding his television return!

General Hospital spoilers for Wednesday, April 24:

In today’s, General Hospital recap, a health scare for Gregory causes wedding panic, and Jason promises to protect Dex for Joss.

Will the wedding hit a snag when Chase and Finn discover that their father is having a medical crisis?

Sam can’t help but worry about Danny and how he’s coping with recent events.

Josslyn has a revealing conversation with Jason.

Could Jordan and Drew, who spend a little time together, be realizing that they are both very attractive single people?

Even as he prepares to start his new job, Dex can’t help wondering if he’s made the right decision.

General Hospital spoilers for Thursday, April 25:

In today’s, General Hospital recap, Jason threatens Sonny over Dex, and Nina warns Kristina about Ava.

Sonny’s having a heckuva week. He started it by getting into a fight with Carly, and now Jason is getting in his face.

TJ has recently been expressing — if only to Marshall — concern about the decision to let Kristina be his and Molly’s surrogate. Those fears are revisited in the wake of Kristina’s latest actions. Meanwhile, Kristina finds herself increasingly worried about her dad.

Drew and Carly share a moment… but not necessarily the kind that will lead to a reunion.

Trina may have lost Spencer, but she manages to stay close to his family as she and Laura care for Ace.

General Hospital spoilers for Friday, April 26:

They’ve navigated the tricky waters of a difficult friendship for years, so what prompts Laura to decide that she can’t forgive Sonny?

Nina and Carly may not be fond of one another, but they do come together over a mutual concern. Might Nina tell Carly her suspicions regarding Ava?

When Finn and Gregory butt heads, it seems safe to assume it might well be over the older man’s medical condition and how it should be handled moving forward.

Molly turns to Alexis for help.

When Sasha receives an unexpected offer, will she accept?
