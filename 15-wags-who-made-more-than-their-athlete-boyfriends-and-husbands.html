The world of sports and professional athletes is one full of never-ending luxuries, impressive incomes, and truly opulent wealth. With the incomes that professional athletes bring in these days, it's hard to think they could find a wife or girlfriend who makes more than them.

Some of these athletes have made a big name for themselves, even being seen as role models and MVPs, whereas others have not quite caught up t the fame or success like their teammates. Either way, it's hard to imagine a wife or a girlfriend of a professional athlete having a bigger income, especially when their athlete boy-toy is very well known. But these ladies give them a run for the money. As for the others, they're more likely to count their blessings for having such a valuable partner to keep them from going hungry after retirement.

As some of these entries may not be all that surprising, some, on the other hand, may really catch your eye. Get ready for a few surprises because here are 15 WAGs who made more than their boyfriends and husbands. Girl power!

Tim Hasselbeck was basically just a backup quarterback when he was playing in the NFL. The former player had a seven year run with the National Football League, playing for six different teams, and only starting for one of them. He caught his big break to be a starting quarterback for The Washington Redskins, but he didn't do too well and Hasselbeck had himself throwing more interceptions than making touchdowns. Yikes.

The two didn't last very long, but it was still a relationship where the girlfriend of an athlete had a higher cash flow than her partner. Nowadays, Jenna's net worth is rumoured to be upwards of $30 million, compared to Ortiz's $15 million.

Everyone is aware of the empire that the Kardashian name has amassed and the constant cash flow it brings in, making Khloe one of the more famous (ex) wives when it comes to sports and athletes.
