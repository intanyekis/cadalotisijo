VALORANT: How to get a smiley face crosshair in the game

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, June 15, 2024

The first step to becoming a better VALORANT player will be to improve your aim, and your rank should start to reflect this. While your main attention will be on developing your muscle memory to improve your aim, having a comfortable crosshair can also make it simpler to score headshots. Small but not too small is ideal for a crosshair.

The smiley face crosshair is a fun alternative that is the opposite of how a crosshair should be in VALORANT, and it should also be clearly visible. If you need more information about VALORANT: How to get a smiley face crosshair in the game, then read carefully, and don’t forget to share with your friends.

VALORANT: How to get a smiley face crosshair in the game

The adaptable system in Valorant enables users to unleash their imaginations and create the zaniest-looking crosshair designs. Even though the majority of uncommon patterns aren’t effective in competitive play, they can be mastered well enough to pick significant frags.

Given how amusing it appears, most players joke about using this crosshair, and beating your opponents while grinning makes it feel even better. Players can experiment with original ideas thanks to the crosshair editor in the game. Here’s how to get VALORANT’s smiley face crosshair.

  • Color: Yellow or Green
  • Outlines: On
  • Outline Opacity: One
  • Outline Thickness: Two
  • Center Dot: On
  • Center Dot Opacity: Zero
  • Center Dot Thickness: Three
  • Show Inner Lines: On
  • Inner Line Opacity: One
  • Inner Line Length: Two
  • Inner Line Thickness: 10
  • Inner Line Offset: Two
  • Movement Error: Off
  • Firing Error: Off
  • Outer Lines: Off

But you’ll need to look further if you’re wondering what the best crosshair in VALORANT is. Finding out what professional players value in their crosshairs by looking at the crosshair settings of players like Yay can be a great place to start. Check out the crosshairs of your favourite players on their streams or YouTube videos.

While everyone will enjoy one’s Valorant matches more thanks to the smiley face design, it will be more difficult for the user to aim precisely than with a clear crosshair design. It could be confusing and is bigger than ideal. Therefore, using the smiley face during placement games is not recommended.

Once you start experimenting with their successful formulas, you can add a touch of your own by adjusting their size, color, or any other aspect of them that you feel can be improved. You can either try to recreate their crosshair settings or search for them.

Players can further customize their profiles with Valorant’s most recent crosshair system. The reticle’s vertical and horizontal lines can now be customized separately, and up to 15 unique profiles can be saved. Over the past few weeks, a number of original crosshair designs have emerged as a result of these new features. Having said that, many players will continue to hold a special place in their hearts for the smiley face.
