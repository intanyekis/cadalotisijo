Tyrese Files Motion For New Judge In Divorce Proceedings, Claims Bias

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, August 4, 2024

Tyrese has filed a second motion to recuse Judge Kevin M. Farmer, who’s handling his divorce, over claims of bias and “collusion.”

According to TMZ and court documents the singer shared on his Instagram, Judge Farmer reportedly cancelled Tyrese’s court hearing without ample notice and requested he pay a $450,000 bond within 10 days of his appeal without allowing the father of two to explain why it’d be impossible to round up that cash so quickly.

In an hour-long Instagram Live, Tyrese shared his beliefs that his estranged wife, Samantha, only divorced him as a cash-grab, fueling his claims that Farmer and Samantha’s legal team are conspiring against him. “You might not like me, but I’m always going to tell the truth and I’m not into play. Victim gaslight manipulate. I actually don’t care about who likes me. God has put me in a position to tell the truth,” said the 44-year-old.

He later called Judge Farmer “a racist” who has “aggressive, biased, egregious and abusive power.” Allegedly, Judge Farmer’s misconduct dates back years and Tyrese feels there should be repercussions for the judge “shutting you down, telling you to shut up, threatening to put you in jail.”

In regards to his tirade, Tyrese noted, “You know what the beauty of social media is? The social media has given us a voice, given us an opportunity to shed light on illegal injustices, and I want y’all to know I never came into this divorce trial to win. I wanted to be married for the rest of my life. I came into this thing to seek justice. I have a prenuptial agreement in place for a reason. She filed for divorce. All of the money that I was supposed to give her in my prenuptial agreement, I gave it to her. Everything that I was supposed to do on behalf of my child, I have done it. This judge is abusing his power. He is literally making s**t up along the way and dropping it on me.”

The “Sweet Lady” singer revealed that his latest rant was less about his divorce and more about him exposing Judge Farmer’s alleged bias against him.

He concluded the Live by saying that his “prenuptial agreement will stand up in court and everything about what Judge Farmer did and is doing, it’s all going to be exposed and it’s all going to be appealed. I believe it with every fiber in my body and the name of Jesus. Watch and see.”
