A Brief Michelle Fairley Wiki Biography

Michelle Fairley, born on 17 January 1964, is a renowned Northern Irish actress. She is well-recognized for her outstanding performances as Catelyn Stark in the HBO series Game of Thrones, Dr. Ava Hessington in multiple episodes of the USA Network series Suits, and as the terrorist Margot Al-Harazi, the primary antagonist in 24: Live Another Day.
