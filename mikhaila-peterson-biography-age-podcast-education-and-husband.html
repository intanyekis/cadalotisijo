Mikhaila Peterson Biography; Age, Podcast, Education And Husband

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, September 8, 2024

Mikhaila Peterson is a popular Canadian blogger who is best known as the Daughter of Jordan Peterson who is a Canadian clinical psychologist.

Mikhaila Peterson was born on 4 January 1992 in Toronto, Canada. She is the daughter of Jordan Peterson and Tammy Roberts. Her father is a renowned clinical psychologist. She has a brother called Julian Peterson.

Mikhaila Peterson was diagnosed with severe joint pains and severe depression and type two bipolar disease when she was still young.

Mikhaila Peterson started her career as a fitness blogger and diet expert. She runs personalized diet and exercises consultations on her website.
