Thomas Dekker Family

Dekker was born in Las Vegas, Nevada to his parents Hilary Dekker  (mother) and David John Ellis Dekker (father). His mother, Hilary, is a concert pianist, acting coach, actor, and singer, and his late father, David John Ellis Dekker, was an artist, set designer, opera singer, and actor. His mother is Welsh and his father was American, with English and Dutch ancestry.

His maternal grandfather was Alun Williams, a radio broadcaster for the British Broadcasting Corporation. As a child, he and his parents moved all over the world, including his mother’s native the United Kingdom, and Canada. He has two step-siblings, Diana and Erik.
