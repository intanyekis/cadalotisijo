Fans React To Emily Ratajkowski Ditching Her Engagement Rings And Repurposing Them Into Divorce Ring

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, July 22, 2024

Highlights

  • Emily Ratajkowski repurposed her engagement rings into "divorce rings" to reflect her current relationship status.
  • Fans have praised the idea of divorce rings, expressing admiration for their design and concept.
  • Ratajkowski has been open about her divorce and her journey towards self-discovery.

Emily Ratajkowski and her ex-husband, Sebastian Bear-McClard, split in July 2022. But rather than get rid of the rings he gave her, she has decided to repurpose them into something that reflects her current relationship status. Enter "divorce rings," as Ratajkowski likes to call them. The rings were created by New York City jewelry brand Alison Lou, who posted them on Instagram and tagged the model.

"A little BTS: We repurposed Emily's original, iconic two-stone engagement ring, that left a mark on the industry, and created these two beauties," the caption of the video, which shows the process of transforming the rings. "As inspiring as her engagement ring was, we cannot wait to see the evolution of Divorce Rings."

What Is The Reaction To Emily Ratajkowski's New Jewelry?

Fans of Ratajkowski have commented on the video and shared their thoughts about the concept of divorce rings. Many feel it is a brilliant idea. "I've been wearing my divorce ring for years! Marriage wasn't forever, but the diamonds sure are," one Instagram user commented. "So good, let's quit this 'throw your ring into a water' thing," another shared. Other comments from fans referred to the rings as "stunning," "wow," and the "coolest idea ever."

Ratajkowski has been divorced for almost two years now after marrying Bear-McClard in a court ceremony in 2018. She has been open about her split and the reasons for it. There was also speculation that her husband cheated on her, with sources telling Page Six, "Yeah, he cheated. He's a serial cheater. It's gross. He's a dog."

"I didn't have the courage to leave for a long time," she said in an interview on the Going Mental podcast in 2023. "I was 100 pounds, and I'd just had a baby, and like, I got really skinny because I was not OK. I tried everything else. I tried to take antidepressants. I was sure that something was wrong with me." In the same interview, she also discussed how she was happy to be divorced, saying it was "so nice to come back to myself, and return back to how I see the world, how I understand things, my instincts, whatever."

What Has Emily Ratajkowski Said About Her Divorce?

When Joe Jonas and Sophie Turner's divorce was announced, Ratajkowski commented on the trend of women getting divorced at a young age. "So, it seems that a lot of young ladies are getting divorced before they turn 30," Ratajkowski said in a September 2023 TikTok video. "As someone who got married at 26 [who] has been separated for a little over a year [at] 32, I have to tell you, I don't think there's anything better. Being in your 20s is the trenches," she added.
