Marietta DePrima Wiki: Salary, Married, Wedding, Spouse, Family

Marietta DePrima (born May 3, 1964) is an American TV actress best known for playing the role Sally Rogers, opposite her television husband, veteran actor, Eric Allen Kramer, on the ABC/UPN sitcom, The Hughleys.DePrima and her husband, TV actor George Newbern, both graduated from Northwestern University in 1986 with degrees in Radio/TV/Film. They were married in 1990, and have three children - Emma (b. 26 June 1995), Mae (b. 19th September 1998), and Ben (b. 15 November 2003). Marietta is a graduate of Carmel Catholic High School in Mundelein, IL and Saint Joseph Elementary School in Libertyville, IL.DePrima has appeared in many television programs including Family Ties, Tour of Duty, Quantum Leap, Boston Legal, Dear John, and Matlock. She played Ariel in a Disney live-action puppet show called Little Mermaid's Island. ﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿
