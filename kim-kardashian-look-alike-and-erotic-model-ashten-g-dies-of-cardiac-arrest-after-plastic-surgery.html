Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, July 8, 2024

Heartbreaking news as Ashten G, known for her resemblance to Kim Kardashian and her work as an erotic model, has passed away due to cardiac arrest following a plastic surgery procedure.

Reports indicate that Christina Ashten Gourkani, popularly known as Ashten G in the erotic streaming media, died in the hospital on April 20 during her recovery from plastic surgery.

Throughout her career, the 34-year-old model shared her stunning pictures on her Instagram page, where she had amassed a following of 618,000 loyal fans.

Her family has announced that her funeral will be held next week and has initiated a GoFundMe page to raise funds for her burial.

“It is with great sorrow and an incredibly heavy heart that we share the most shattering, unfortunate, and unexpected passing of our beloved daughter and sister, Christina Ashten Gourkani,” expressed her family on the GoFundMe page.

“We received a distressing phone call from a family member, frantically screaming and crying about Gourkani’s deteriorating condition, a call that shattered our world instantly and will haunt our family forever,” the statement continued.

“Her sudden and tragic passing is currently under investigation as a homicide linked to a medical procedure that took a turn for the worse,” claimed the family.
