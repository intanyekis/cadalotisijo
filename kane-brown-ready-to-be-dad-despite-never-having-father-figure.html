Kane Brown Ready to Be Dad Despite Never Having Father Figure

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, August 5, 2024

Preparing to be a parent! Kane Brown is getting ready to welcome his baby girl with his wife, Katelyn Jae.

“I think I’m going to be a great dad,” the country singer, 25, told Us Weekly exclusively at the CMT Awards on Wednesday, June 5. “I love kids and I grew up with all females. I’m having a girl. So, I feel like I know most of it. Hopefully I don’t get surprised too much. And I love my dad, but I haven’t really had a father figure … so I’m going to try and do everything that I can to be the best that I can be.”

The “Good as You” singer added: “I’m going to be the spoiler parent. [Jae will] probably be a spoiler too, but we’re going to have boundaries.”

In April, the American Music Award winner announced that the Berklee College of Music grad, 26, is pregnant by posting a sonogram on Instagram. “It’s been so hard keeping this a secret!!!!! But the KB’S ARE GONNA BE PARENTS!!! KB3,” he captioned the social media upload. “IM GONNA BE A DADDY!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!”

Jae shared a video from the pair’s ultrasound appointment at the time. “The coolest day of my life by far,” the Philadelphia native wrote. “I barely could stop laughing / crying to let her get pictures but I am soooooo happy and excited. @kanebrown_music I love you so much & am so excited to have an addition to our family to share our everyday fun, travel & love with.”
