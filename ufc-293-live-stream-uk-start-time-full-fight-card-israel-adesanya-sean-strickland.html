UFC 293 LIVE: UK start time, full fight card and how to follow as Israel Adesanya defends title agai

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, May 31, 2024

Middleweight champion Israel Adesanya will defend his title against Sean Strickland in the main event of UFC 293 tonight.

Adesanya won his belt back against Alex Pereira last time out as he got his revenge on the Brazilian with a second-round knockout in April. 

And ‘The Last Style Bender’ has since been pushing for a fight against Strickland with the leading contender for the title, Dricus du Plessis, unable to fight.

‘Tarzan’ has won his last two bouts, beating Nassourdine Imavov and Abusupiyan Magomedov and is now set for a shot a becoming champion.

Elsewhere on the card, Tai Tauisava takes on Alexander Volkov in a huge heavyweight clash which will co-headline the event. 

UFC 293: Date and how to follow 

UFC 293 will take place on Saturday, September 9 at the Qudos Bank Arena in Sydney, Australia.

“This f****** cringe-lord. F****** A. Even talking about him makes me want to go f****** take a shower. I feel dirty.”
