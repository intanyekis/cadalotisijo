Girlfriend / Spouse

Plácido has dated –

  • Ana María Guerra Cué (1957–1961) – Plácido had married Ana María Guerra Cué on August 29, 1957, when he was just 16 years old. She was a piano student at that time. They had a son together named José Plácido Domingo Guerra (b. June 16, 1958) (Photographer). The couple had parted ways in 1961. Ana passed away in 2006. Through José, he has a granddaughter named Ivonne Armant (Actress)
  • Marta Ornelas (1962-Present) – Plácido had started dating Mexican opera soprano singer, stage director, and designer Marta Ornelas in 1962, and they got married later that year, on August 1, 1962. They have 2 sons together named Plácido Francisco aka Plácido Domingo Jr. (b. October 21, 1965) (Singer, Songwriter, Record Producer) and Alvaro Maurizio Domingo (b. October 11, 1968).  Marta had been voted the ‘Mexican Singer of the Year’ in 1962 and the couple had performed together frequently after their marriage. However, she gave up her promising career after her first pregnancy to devote herself to taking care of the family. Plácido has 3 granddaughters through Plácido Francisco.
