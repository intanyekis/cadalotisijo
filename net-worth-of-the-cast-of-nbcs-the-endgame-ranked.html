Net Worth Of The Cast Of NBC's 'The Endgame', Ranked

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, June 14, 2024

Quick Links

  • Karl Josef Co ($1 million)
  • Jordan Johnson-Hinds ($1 million)
  • Costa Ronin ($1.5 million)
  • Kamal Bolden ($1.5 million)
  • Noah Bean ($2 million)
  • Mark Damon Espinoza ($2.5 million)
  • Ryan Michelle Bathé ($4 million)
  • Morena Baccarin ($8 million)

A unique crime thriller that premiered on NBC on February 21, 2022, The Endgame is a creation of Jake Coburn and Emmy-winning television writer and creator Nicholas Wootton best known for the shows Prison Break, NYPD Blue, and Law & Order. The Endgame revolves around the story of a criminal mastermind Elena Federova who goes against the FBI led by principal-agent Val Turner. The heist drama is a tale about an unlikely partnership filled with mystery and hard-hitting explosions. With each episode airing on Monday nights, it has become one of the most highly anticipated shows to start a weekday night.

Featuring a stunning cast that includes a combination of young and seasoned actors, The Endgame is a star-studded show shining a limelight on its characters that will slowly rise to popularity with each passing episode.

Karl Josef Co ($1 million)

A musical theater artist and actor, Karl Josef Co has amassed a $1 million fortune playing supporting characters in various television shows and being part of musicals. In The Endgame, he stars as Louie, a charming and confident master thief who helps the lead antagonist in her schemes, as noted by Variety. Co has guest-starred in notable shows such as Madam Secretary and Unbreakable Kimmy Schmidt.

Jordan Johnson-Hinds ($1 million)

Hailing from Ontario, Canada, Jordan Johnson-Hinds was inspired by Tyler Perry to pursue acting. The actor has made an appearance in the movie Robocop and starred in the television show Nurses for two seasons which went off the air in 2021. He stars as FBI Agent Anthony Flowers in The Endgame, who works alongside the principal agent and lead protagonist of the movie. Through his acting credits, Johnson-Hinds has garnered a $1 million net worth.

Costa Ronin ($1.5 million)

Russian-Australian actor Costa Ronin is an acclaimed actor and cinematographer who has worked in several movies and television shows, amassing a $1.5 million net worth. He stars as Sergey Vodianov, the lead antagonist's lover and partner in crime in the latest show. According to Deadline, Ronin’s character on the show spent decades building their criminal empire, and Vodianov is presumed to be dead at the start of the show.

Kamal Bolden ($1.5 million)

Kamal Bolden plays the character of Owen Turner in the series and has worked in several productions before landing a role in the NBC show. He has collected a $1.5 million fortune working in projects such as Keys To The City, Vacation Friends, and The Resident. While starring in The Endgame, he will also appear in the BBC drama series 61st Street.

Noah Bean ($2 million)

Playing an agent in The Endgame, Noah Bean portrays the character of Jonathan Doak, who believes in pursuing justice and capturing the criminals by following the rules. Bean has starred in several notable shows, including Nikita, 12 Monkeys, and Vinyl, earning him a $2 million net worth. More recently, he was a supporting actor in the critically acclaimed biographical drama King Richard, starring alongside Academy-Award nominated actor Will Smith, as reported by Cinema Blend.

Mark Damon Espinoza ($2.5 million)

Mark Damon Espinoza has had a prolific career in movies and television shows that have helped him gain a $2.5 million net worth. The actor plays the character of Rogelio Réal in The Endgame. Espinoza made his feature debut in 1993 in The Fugitive and starred in famous productions such as Beverly Hills 90210. He earned recognition after playing supporting roles in long-running soap operas such as The Young And The Restless and The Bold And The Beautiful.

Ryan Michelle Bathé ($4 million)

Leading the cast in the series as the protagonist, Ryan Michelle Bathé, as FBI Agent Val Turner. As mentioned by The Hollywood Reporter, Val Turner is on her first FBI case and is one of the many people who has crossed paths with the antagonist before. Bathé has worked in several high-profile television shows, including This Is Us and Boston Legal. She has amassed a $4 million fortune through her diligent work in Hollywood.

Morena Baccarin ($8 million)

The antagonist of the latest NBC show, Morena Baccarin, livened the show with her charm and stunning acting skills. She portrays the lead role of Elena Federova, a captured international arms dealer who has planned several coordinated bank heists. Before starring in the show, Baccarin has been part of several fan-favorite movies and shows, including the Deadpool series that has grossed over a billion dollars at the box office. The actress has made an $8 million fortune from her acting credits.

A layered show that unboxes a new scheme and mystery with every episode, The Endgame is one of the most engaging shows on television at the moment. The cat-and-mouse thriller with a stellar cast has kept everyone on their toes for each new episode. Currently airing on NBC and the Peacock streaming service, new episodes are released on Monday nights.
