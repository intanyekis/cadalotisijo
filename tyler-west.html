Tyler West is an English television and radio presenter who started his career as a presenter for CBBC in December 2012. As of February 2023, Tyler had been presenting on weekday afternoons (4-7 pm) on Kiss FM. He gained further prominence after being featured as a contestant on the 20th series of Strictly Come Dancing.
