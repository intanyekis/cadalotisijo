    Letícia Bufoni Facts

  • Bufoni was just 9 years old when she began skating.
  • It was her grandmother who gifted Leticia her first skateboard at the age of 11.
  • Bufoni grew up in the streets playing sports.
  • Her father was against her decision to take up skateboarding and broke her board more than once.
  • At the age of 14, Leticia shifted to the United States along with her older friends. The same year, Bufoni competed at her first X Games in Los Angeles in 2007.
  • From 2010-2013, Letícia was ranked the #1 women’s street skateboarder by the “World Cup of Skateboarding” four years in a row.
  • She held The Guinness Book of World Records (2017) for the “Most Wins Of The World Cup of Skateboarding.”
  • Bufoni became an American citizen on April 7, 2021.
  • She has a pet dog named Summer Bufoni.
