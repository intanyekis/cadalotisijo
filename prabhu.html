                          Some Lesser Known Facts About Prabhu

  • Does Prabhu smoke?: Not Known
  • Does Prabhu drink alcohol?: Not Known
  • He was born into a filmy background in Chennai. His father was an actor and his elder brother is a film producer. 
  • Prabhu started his film career as an executive producer and he used to work under his uncle V. C. Shanmugam.
  • He made his film debut in a remake of a Hindi film Kalicharan in 1982.
  • He was a sports enthusiast in his childhood. He loved to play Football, Cricket, and Hockey. He won several sports and cultural competition at his school.
  • He was awarded the Tamil Nadu State Best Actor Award twice respectively in 1991 and 2001.
  • He predominantly works in Tamil Films. He has also worked in a few Malayalam, Kannada, and Telugu films.
  • He has worked in more than 150 films in his three decades of acting career.
  • Despite being married, Prabhu married again to actress Khushboo on 12 September 1993 but they both separated after four months of their marriage.
