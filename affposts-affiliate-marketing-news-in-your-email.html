AffPosts Affiliate Marketing News in Your Email

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, July 1, 2024

A while back I wrote a post called “Where do you get your daily affiliate marketing fix?“, that features a bunch of sites that offer data feeds of all the latest blog updates in the affiliate marketing space. Basically giving you a few sources on where to find the most recent news in the affiliate marketing niche.

If you aren’t using a syndicated news site, there are plenty of ways to read up on the latest trends in affiliate marketing that you might already be using:

  • Individually visiting your favorite affiliate marketing blogs
  • Getting updates through social networks
  • Word of mouth from friends and co-workers
  • Subscribe to specific blog newsletters and rss feeds

The problem with all of these methods is that they not only take take, but it’s quite a task to visit all of your favorite sites and knowing when they were last updated.

Thanks to a new site called Affposts.com, created by Luke from Peerfly, the process for finding the latest news and blog posts is a lot easier. One glance at the site and you will quickly be able to see what’s hot in the affiliate marketing blogosphere.

The benefits to using the site is that you will get to see a quick view of the latest 10 posts that went live from the many sites that are being covered across the site.

One of the perks I like about the site is subscribing to the newsletter and getting a single email every day of the top posts and which blogs they came from. This makes it easy for me to quickly browse through the list and visit any of the full posts that interest me.

Be sure to check it out and subscribe to the mailing list. If you don’t currently have a home page for your browser and love getting the latest affiliate news, this is a great site to consider.
